Rainy days

1 Comment

Up-Close

This is just a random thought of the day that I’d like to share:

Have you ever thought about everything you hate about rainy days? Getting up at 6am and it’s gloomy as hell. Body aches from the low pressure. Or even just getting your pants/socks/hair wet. You just know that it’s going to be a really shitty day. Lately, the weather has been teasing Torontonians to the max! A few nice sunny days with a high of 20+ degrees celsius… and then BOOM. A blast of thunder showers for 3 days! Ta Daaa!

Yesterday, like every other day, I was on my way home from work: Exhausted on the streetcar, sleeping on the subway, becoming deaf on the RT, and getting crushed on the bus (I really make use of the TTC system, ha!). I finally get picked up and as I’m in the car staring out the window, I thought to myself… “damn, the grass is green”. And you know what? Without that rain, that grass wouldn’t have been so green. And those sunny days wouldn’t have look so beautiful without that green ass grass! That thought made me smile, and then I proceeded to sing that “Raindrops keep falling on my head” song. That is the story of how I made my own day better : )

The End.
