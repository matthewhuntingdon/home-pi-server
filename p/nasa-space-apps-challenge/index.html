NASA Space Apps Challenge

Branding, Web Design

My team competed in and won 1st place in Toronto's NASA Space Apps Challenge, a 48-hour challenge to design and develop an app that can be used as a learning tool to bring more awareness and educate people about NASA and reinvigorate people’s interest in space.

Designed a story-telling app of The Voyager 1’s exploration, presented facts at every milestone in the Voyager 1’s journey in an aesthetically pleasing and easy-to-digest manner through use of elements such as iconography and type treatment.
