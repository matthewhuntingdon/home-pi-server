Charlotte Tang » Music http://charlottetang.com Interactive Design Ninja Extraordinaire Fri, 08 May 2015 02:43:59 +0000 en hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=3.3.2 I’m Ready for The (Rocket) Summer! http://charlottetang.com/im-ready-for-the-rocket-summer/ http://charlottetang.com/im-ready-for-the-rocket-summer/#comments Thu, 03 Jun 2010 19:30:42 +0000 Charlotte http://charlottetang.com/?p=231 How is it that I’m four months late in discovering The Rocket Summer‘s Of Men and Angels Album?!

This is awesome. This album retains the catchy driving rhythms and melodies from his previous album, Do You Feel? (which I LOVED!). I haven’t even finished listening to the entire album yet, but so far my faves are “Hills and Valleys”, “Walls” and “Let you go”, which features an majestic piano melody that is to DIE for! I wish I could find the sheet music for his songs. I still find it unbelievable that he composes and plays all the instruments himself. Watch this video of Bryce in the studio doing his thang, and prepared to be amazed.

Well done, Bryce, another excellent album just in time for fun filled summer days.
